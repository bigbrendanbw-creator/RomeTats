The Ink

I'm a tattoo artist out of a private studio in Clearlake, California. Black and grey realism is what I'm known for — horror, heavy portraits, the stuff that takes real time and real focus to get right.

I do it all, but this is where my heart is. Every piece I do is custom. You come in with an idea, we build something together, and it gets done right.
